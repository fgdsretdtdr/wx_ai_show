东施效颦

The beautiful woman Xi Shi was ill, frowning and pressing her heart. The ugly woman in the same village saw it and felt that her posture was beautiful. She also looked like her, but she was terribly ugly.

  • 东施效颦
  • dōng shī xiào pín
  • 美丽的西施生病了，皱着眉头，按着心口。
  • 同村的丑女人看见了，觉得她的姿势很美。
  • 她也模仿她的样子，但是非常难看。
